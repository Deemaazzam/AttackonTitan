Story

The story is set in a world where humanity is forced to live within three massive walls, known as Wall Maria, Wall Rose, and Wall Sina, which protect them from Titans, giant humanoid creatures who devour humans for seemingly no reason. The Titans come in a variety of sizes and shapes, but all have one thing in common: their insatiable hunger for human flesh. The main protagonist of the story is Eren Yeager , a young boy who lives with his parents in the outermost district of Wall Maria. One day, a colossal Titan appears and destroys the gate of Wall Maria, allowing a horde of smaller Titans to enter and wreak havoc on the city. Eren's mother is killed in the attack, and he swears to get revenge by killing all the Titans. Several years later, Eren, along with his adopted sister Mikasa Ackerman and best friend Armin Arlert, join the military as part of the Survey Corps, a branch of the military tasked with exploring outside the walls and gathering information about the Titans. Eren discovers that he has the ability to transform into a Titan, a rare power that is not well understood. This ability puts him in danger, as some members of the military view him as a threat. As the story progresses, Eren and his friends uncover the truth about the Titans and the world they live in. They learn that the Titans were created by a mysterious race of people called the Founding Titans, who have the ability to control them. The Founding Titans are also responsible for creating the walls that protect humanity. The Survey Corps eventually discovers that the ruling government has been keeping secrets about the true nature of the Titans and the history of humanity. Eren's father had injected him with a serum that would allow him to access the Founding Titan's power and memories. Eren uses this power to learn about the past and uncover the government's secrets.

As the story progresses, Eren and his friends face numerous battles against the Titans, some of which result in the deaths of beloved characters. They encounter various factions within the military and government who have their own agendas and motives, leading to complicated political and moral dilemmas. The story also explores themes of power, corruption, and the lengths people will go to in order to protect their own interests. The characters struggle to navigate their own morals and beliefs as they fight against both Titans and their fellow humans. Throughout the series, the stakes become higher and the battle between humanity and the Titans intensifies. The Titans are not mindless monsters, and some even display intelligence and human-like behavior. The characters also encounter new types of Titans, such as the Beast Titan and the Female Titan, who possess unique abilities and characteristics. To You,In 2000 Years

Legend has it that in the year -1003, The story of the Titans begins with the first Titan,a young girl named Ymir,once lived a provincial life in a small village. Everything changed when a warrior tribe called the Eldians attacked.The villagers were either killed or enslaved.One day, Ymir let her masters' pigs escape. The Eldian King Fritz set her "free" but commanded his warriors to hunt her. The injured Ymir took refuge in an enormous tree,where a creature, resembling a cross between a centipede and disembodied nervous system, bonded with her,turning her into the first Titan. With this power/curse, Ymir was able to overthrow all of her enemies, who at that time were grouped in the well-known Marley Empire.13 years after Ymir became a titan,she was killed saving Fritz from assassination by Marleyans .To preserve her power,Fritz fed her remains to their daughters. Filtered through generations,nine distint titans emerged. Ymir's own power became called the Founding Titan and was passed down through her and Fritz's direct descendants;only those of royal blood can wield its powers. The Titans left their people in an era of prosperity thanks to the use of this magical power. From the year -989 to 710 was a dark time for humanity. The great power granted by the Nine Titans made their wielders believe they had the right to dominate different parts of the world, steal their possessions, and even choose some humans to mate with. Thus was born the Ackerman family (to which both Levi and Mikasa would belong many years later). From the beginning, they were brainwashed to protect the bearer of the Founder Titan, something we will see in the aforementioned protagonist's behavior towards Eren from the beginning of the anime. The Ackerman, who were characterized by their incredible fighting skills, were eventually joined by the Asians of the Hizuru clan (led by the Azumabito family), who had a lot of dealings with the royal family that passed the Founder Titan from generation to generation.

Fall of the Eldians

Karl fritz, the 145th Eldian King,was aghast by his people's legacy of violence. To repent,he decides to orchestrtate his own empire's fall.The king conspires in secret with the Tybur family,holders of the WarHammer Titan.Fritz and the Tybur fabricated a story about a Marleyan named "Helos" slaying "The Devil of all Earth".Fritz then took the royal family and many other Eldians to the island Paradise.Using the Founder's power, he creates millions of Colossal Titans who together formed the walls. To amke sure no Eldians venture outside the walls,Fritz erases his people's collective memory,making them believe they're the last of humanity.

Meanwhile, the royal family goes underground, placing a puppet king on the throne while they rule in secret as the "Reiss" family. For the next century, the Founding Titan and the memories of its holders are passed down through the Reiss family. Its holders thus inherit not just Karl Fritz's power, but also his pacifist ideology, ensuring that none of them ever set lose the Wall Titans to rebuild the Empire.

Outside The walls

While the world stayed static within Paradis' walls, it didn't outside. With Marley now in possession of seven out of the nine Titans, they become the world's new superpower. Helos and the Devil of All Earth go from myth to history, while the Eldians left outside Paradis are pushed into internment zones, forced to wear identifying armbands, and treated as second-class citizens at best and vermin at worst.The Marleyans keep the Eldians around for use in their Warrior program; Eldian children who inherit Titan powers and serve in Marley's military earn citizenship for themselves and their families (the War Hammer remains in the Tyburs' control). Defiant Eldians are punished by being sent to Paradis Island and transformed into the Pure Titans menacing their cousins inside the wall; in 785, Eldians called "the Cult of Ymir," including a young girl named after the Founder, suffer this fate.

In 806, Eldian Grisha Yeager is born in the Liberio internment zone. In 817, his younger sister Fay (born 809) is killed by Marleyans guards, pushing Grisha to join the "Eldian Restorationist" movement in 824. Grisha marries Dina Fritz, the last royal Eldian outside the walls, and has a son with her, Zeke, in 825. The Yeagers push Zeke to become a warrior, but at the advice of his mentor Tom Ksaver, Zeke sells his parents out in 832.

All the Restorationists bar Grisha are made into Titans on Paradis. Grisha is saved by Marleyan officer Kruger, a secret Eldian and holder of the Attack Titan. Kruger passes the Attack Titan to Grisha and sends him to the Walls to claim the Founding Titan from the Reiss family. Between 832 and 835, Grisha marries Carla Yeager and becomes the doctor in Shiganshina, an outlying district of Wall Maria.

New Warriors

Back in Marley, the government begins to plot the invasion of Paradis, both to claim the Founding Titan and the island's resources. Zeke inherits the Beast Titan from Ksaver in 842, and leads the next generation of Warriors, selected the following year: Annie Leonhart the Female Titan (born 834), Reiner Braun the Armored Titan (born 833), Bertholdt Hoover,the Colossal Titan, Pieck Finger the Cart Titan, and Marcel Galliard the Jaw Titan. In 845, Reiner, Annie, Bertholdt, and Marcel are sent to Paradis to begin the invasion. Before they reach the walls, the pure Titan that was once Ymir attacks them and eats Marcel. Ymir inherits the power of the Jaw Titan and regains her human form, but the Warriors run away before they can witness this. Annie and Bertholdt consider abandoning the mission but Reiner implores them to push forward.

Transforming into The colossal Titan,Bertholdt breaks down the wall of Shiganshina. and there's when the plot of "Attack on Titan" begins.
