Fan Theories

  • There are many fan theories surrounding the Attack on Titan series. Some of the most popular theories include:

    • Eren is manipulating events from the future: This theory suggests that Eren is using his power as the Attack Titan to manipulate events in the present in order to change the outcome of the future.

    • Mikasa is an Ackerman hybrid: This theory proposes that Mikasa is a hybrid of the Ackerman and Asian bloodlines, which gives her enhanced abilities and immunity to the Founding Titan's mind control.

    • Armin is the true hero of the story: This theory suggests that Armin, rather than Eren, is the true hero of the story, and that he will ultimately be the one to save humanity.

    • the final chapter will have a time loop: This theory proposes that the final chapter of the series will feature a time loop, in which the characters are trapped in a cycle of events that they are unable to break.

    • the series is a commentary on war and nationalism: This theory suggests that the series is a commentary on the dangers of war and nationalism, and that it is meant to be a cautionary tale

    • Titans subconsciously know eating a Titan shifter will revert them back to human form.They eat every person in the hopes they'll turn back, even though they don't seem to have any real drive or motivation.
